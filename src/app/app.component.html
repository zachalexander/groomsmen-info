Suit Rental and Attire Information

The Black Tux

After a lot of research and discussion with multiple suit/tuxedo rental places, I’ve decided to go with The Black Tux as the suit rental company of choice for the wedding.

I did this for a couple of reasons:

  • Since people are living on different parts of the globe, I thought having a company with a strong online presence would be beneficial.
  • They have multiple locations at Nordstrom outlet stores around the U.S., just in case you prefer to go to the store for a fitting.
  • I did a "home try-on" already and the suit felt well-made and the price for the rental was quite reasonable (relatively speaking).

All of this to say, if there are issues with your order, or if you are on a tight financial budget, please do reach out to me. The only thing that really matters is that we are all together, NOT what we are wearing.
